Nest Evac

Get the people out of there!

Select a unit and press Q to evacuate it and get points! Keep in mind, that it will make the monster turn to the direction of the evacuee!

If you want to make the monster go in the reverse direction, select a unit and sacrifice it with W, but keep in mind, that this will cost you some points.

 

Every time the monster eats a captive, you lose some points! The game is lost when you lose 10 units.

 

Press R to restart!
